# Persona 2: Frustrated Team Member

## Scenario
The employee had a challenging day and felt that others did not contribute effectively.

## Path Taken

OPEN_Q → Frustrating  
A1_Q1 → I felt external factors affected how things turned out  
A1_Q2 → I felt the outcome was influenced by factors outside my control  
A2_Q1 → I felt some team members did not contribute effectively  
A2_Q2 → I felt others should have contributed more  
A3_Q1 → I focused on how the team could improve  
A3_Q2 → I focused on the team's overall performance  

## Signals
- Axis 1: External  
- Axis 2: Entitlement  
- Axis 3: Team  

## Final Reflection
You initially described your day as 'Frustrating'. Looking deeper, you leaned external in how you handled situations, entitlement in how you contributed, and team in how you viewed progress.
